What does the cover layer look like?

This is the layer the brush strokes erase — the reveal goes from this to your desk photo underneath. Pick the aesthetic that fits your portfolio best.

Dark void + noise

Deep black with subtle film grain. Brush cuts through to reveal light — high contrast, editorial, dramatic. Think dark room exposure.

canvas underpainting

Canvas / paper texture

Your current aesthetic, but rendered in WebGL. Warm cream with pencil sketch lines. Brush erases it to reveal the photo — familiar but higher fidelity.

Painted glaze / impasto

Colorful painterly overlay — deep blues, purples, ambers. Brush cuts through thick paint, revealing the photo underneath. Closer to the KidSuper aesthetic.

Frosted glass / matte

Blurred, frosted overlay — like fogged glass. Brush clears it to reveal crisp photo beneath. Clean, modern, minimal. Satisfying contrast between fog and sharpness.
