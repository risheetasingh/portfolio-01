How should the brush stroke feel?

This is the character of each stamp the shader places. Fast vs slow movement will affect size/opacity in all options.

gaussian falloff

Soft round (current, upgraded)

Smooth radial gradient stamp. Looks polished, predictable. Your current makeBrush() — but in GLSL it gets noise displacement on the edges for organic softness.

noise-displaced edges

Dry brush / bristle

Turbulence noise displaces the stroke boundary. Edges are ragged, textural — like a dry-loaded brush dragged across canvas. More tactile, more raw.

directional smear

Ink wash / velocity smear

Stroke shape stretches in the direction of mouse movement. Slow = round blob; fast = elongated smear with feathered tail. Feels most alive and responsive.

Note: In all options, moving faster makes a wider/lighter stroke. Moving slowly makes a denser, more opaque reveal. This velocity response is baked into the GLSL shader.
